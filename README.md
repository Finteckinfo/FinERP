## ğŸš€ Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- An Ethereum wallet (MetaMask recommended)
- Small amount of ETH for gas fees (testnets work too!)

### Quick Start

1. **Connect Your Wallet**
   - Click "Connect Wallet" on the login page
   - Approve the connection in your wallet
   - You're in!

2. **Create Your First Project**
   - Click "New Project" in the header
   - Enter project details and budget
   - Your funds are held in escrow

3. **Add Subtasks**
   - Break down your project into tasks
   - Allocate funds to each subtask
   - Assign to team members

4. **Track Progress**
   - Team members update task status
   - Review and approve completed work
   - Funds are released automatically upon approval
